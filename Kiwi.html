KIWI

Kiwi fruit is native to north-central and eastern China.

The most common cultivar group of kiwifruit is oval, about the size of a large hen's egg 5–8 cm in length and 4.5–5.5 cm in diameter.

It has a fibrous, dull greenish-brown skin and bright green or golden flesh with rows of tiny, black, edible seeds. The fruit has a soft texture and a sweet but unique flavor.
