CUSTARD APPLE

There are four main custard apple growing regions, all found on the east coast of Australia.

Custard Apple is full of vitamin C anti-oxidants, which helps to combat many diseases and also enhances the immune system.

Custard apple is abundant with potassium, magnesium and contains vitamin A, calcium, copper, fiber and phosphorous.

It has high calorific value, able to provide sustained energy and delicious in nature. The luscious fruit is eaten throughout the world.
