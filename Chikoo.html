CHIKOO

Chikoo, most commonly known as 'Sapota' in India, is a very familiar fruit.

Chikoo is also called as Naseberry, Mud Apples, and Sapodilla Plum.

Chikoo is a delicate brown fruit which tastes sweet and yummy. Chikoo is scientifically known as 'Sapodilla.' It comes from the Sapotaceae family in Central America.

Price per kg is 150 Rs.
